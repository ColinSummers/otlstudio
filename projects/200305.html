Bookshelf

Pacific Palisades, CA

2003

Occasionally we like a small, contained exercise. This was a design for a divider between a master bedroom and the study area adjacent. We used generating lines from the window heights, the proportions of the room, and figured out some of the lighting issues. It was in an impeccably renovated house in the Riviera section of the Pacific Palisades. Our design, which included a set of center shelves out of enameled steel, was interesting to the clients, but they solved the problem with a piece of furniture from Ikea instead.
