Bonhill (renovation)

Brentwood, CA

1998

344 Bonhill was built by a contractor without an owner. It changed hands frequently until the clients purchased it in 1998. Most of the spaces were too grand for the small family, so we cut the entry hall in half and created a library space.

Upstairs we truncated the absurdly large Master Bathroom and recovered enough space for a writer's office that was hidden behind a mirror.
