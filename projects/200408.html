The Slammer: Motor Court

Las Vegas, NV

2004

The original garage for The Slammer was just two cars. With recumbent bicycles, visitors with their rental cars and (eventually) a family vehicle, it made sense to have more parking available. This also gave us the opportunity to correct the lack of storage in the original house.

By carefully referencing the existing work and the geometry of the entire project, we were able to create a new court that focused on arriving vehicles.

The large garage is built with enough structure that we can add a second story later if we need to. The shaded parking is of a type found in Las Vegas.
