Outside The Lines Studio

After twenty years of designing together, we've taken a moment to make an online presence and a portfolio for potential clients to peruse. Up until now our marketing efforts consisted of the few business cards we would hand out; all of our projects arrive in our studio via word-of-mouth.

We have designed web sites, furniture, rooms, gardens, and assisted with architect-client interaction on houses (the studio includes an architect licensed in Pennsylvania and California). We have been involved in the design of several residences and a couple commercial projects in quite a few different states. The Projects page is a portfolio-in-progress.

The Mission page will tell you what we are hoping to accomplish. About will tell you who we are and how to get in touch.
